# Delete Your Terminal Data

You have two ways to remove all data tied to your device:

## In the app (fastest)

1. Open Terminal.
2. Tap the **Settings** tab.
3. Scroll to **Privacy**.
4. Tap **Delete my data**.
5. Confirm. All queue reports, feedback, and zone contributions
   associated with your device ID are removed from our servers
   immediately.

## By email

Email **lulakcostudios@gmail.com** with your device ID (shown in the
diagnostic info when you submit feedback). We delete the data within
30 days.

## What gets deleted

- All queue reports you submitted
- All feedback messages and ratings
- Your anonymous device ID
- Any zones you created (if no other users have contributed to them)

## What does not get deleted

- Queue zones that other users have also contributed to — these
  remain in the shared airport map. Your individual contribution is
  removed from the aggregate.
